== Description ==

This is not just a plugin, it symbolizes the wild adventure of an entire generation summed up in three words proposed most famously by William Goldman: The Princess Bride - As You Wish. When activated you will randomly see a quote from <cite>The Princess Bride</cite> in the upper right of your admin screen on every page.
